## Overview

This presentation walked through the fundamentals of antenna theory for new Technician and General class operators, with an emphasis on practical takeaways you can act on right away.

## Key Topics

- What makes an antenna resonate — the relationship between frequency and physical length
- Half-wave dipoles: the math, the materials, and a live demonstration
- Feed line options: coax, ladder line, and when each makes sense
- Common mistakes and easy fixes
