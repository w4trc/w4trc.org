## What is SOTA?
Summits on the Air (SOTA) promotes portable operation from qualifying summits. Hike in, set up lightweight gear, and make contacts.

## Gear & safety
- Lightweight HF/VHF rig, small battery, efficient antenna
- Weather-appropriate clothing, water, first-aid kit, navigation
- Tell someone your plan; check access rules

## Get started
1. See **summits.sota.org.uk** for nearby summits.
2. Review activation rules (minimum QSOs, operating within the activation zone).
3. Join an experienced activator on a club outing.
