## About the Net

The W4TRC Digital Experimentation Net meets on the **first Sunday evening of each month at 8:00 PM**. Each session focuses on a different digital mode — giving members hands-on experience beyond standard FM voice. Whether you are new to digital or a seasoned operator, this is a low-pressure way to experiment, ask questions, and compare notes with fellow hams.

- **When:** First Sunday of each month, 8:00 PM local
- **Modes:** Varies each session — FT8, JS8Call, Winlink, APRS, DMR, Fusion, and more
- **All licensed hams welcome** — no prior digital experience required

## Getting Started with Digital Modes

Digital operation can feel intimidating at first, but the basics are more accessible than they look. A typical setup requires only your existing radio, a computer or tablet, and a simple audio interface (or a radio with a built-in sound card like many Icom, Yaesu, and Kenwood models). Software such as WSJT-X, JS8Call, or Winlink Express is free to download.

Net Control will announce the mode and any required software or settings at the start of each session, and we will walk through the process together on the air.
