Parks on the Air (POTA)

We regularly activate local parks as part of the Parks on the Air (POTA) program. These activations provide an opportunity for club members to practice portable operations, improve their operating skills, and enjoy the outdoors while promoting amateur radio.

Summits on the Air (SOTA)

Club members also participate in Summits on the Air (SOTA) activations, where they hike to mountain summits and operate portable stations. SOTA activations challenge operators to adapt to varying terrain and conditions while fostering camaraderie among participants.

More info

Fox Hunting

Fox hunting, or amateur radio direction finding, is another exciting activity that our club members engage in. This activity involves using radio receivers and directional antennas to locate hidden transmitters (the "fox"). Fox hunts are both fun and educational, helping participants hone their technical skills and teamwork.
